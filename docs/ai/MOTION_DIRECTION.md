# Motion Direction

> Generated by Coded by M — Site Architecture System. Source of truth: `/docs/ai/DISCOVERY.md` and `/docs/ai/DECISIONS_LOG.md`.

## Core Motion Concept
"The drawing builds itself." Motion echoes the act of drafting an architectural plan: hairlines **draw themselves** into place, imagery is **revealed** behind moving masks, and the page settles with quiet precision. Silent, deliberate, never decorative or bouncy.

## Motion Language
- **Easing:** custom ease-out / expo-out (e.g. `cubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1)`). No bounce, no overshoot.
- **Duration:** 0.6–1.2s for primary reveals; 0.2–0.35s for microinteractions.
- **Line-draw:** SVG/CSS stroke animation for hairlines on section entry.
- **Image reveal:** clip-path / mask wipe (vertical or horizontal), subtle scale-from-1.05.
- **Scroll:** Lenis smooth scroll; minimal parallax on hairlines and large media only.

## Hierarchy
1. Primary narrative motion — hero hairline draw-in, hero image reveal.
2. Section transitions — lines draw, content fades/translates up slightly on enter.
3. Microinteractions — link/hover hairline underline, card hover meta reveal.
4. Decorative motion — minimal; subtle hairline scroll reaction only.

## Page / Section Priorities
- **Home hero:** hairline grid draws in, statement fades up, featured image reveals. Highest craft.
- **Selected projects:** staggered reveal as they enter viewport; hairline separators draw.
- **Project detail cover:** mask reveal on load; technical sheet lines draw.
- **Project gallery:** gentle reveal per image, preserve LCP for first image (no delay on critical content).
- **Page transitions:** short fade/slide; no heavy route animations that delay content.

## Microinteractions
- Nav/links: hairline underline draws from left on hover.
- Buttons: border/underline hairline animates; no fill flash.
- Project cards: image subtle scale + metadata/hairline reveal on hover.
- Form fields: underline animates to active color on focus.

## Mobile Behavior
Reduce parallax and large reveals; keep simple fade-up and hairline draws. Prioritize performance and tap responsiveness over choreography.

## Accessibility
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`: disable line-draw/parallax/mask reveals, fall back to instant or simple opacity.
- Never gate content visibility on motion completion.

## Performance Restrictions
- Do not compromise LCP, CLS or INP. Critical images render immediately.
- Prefer transform/opacity/clip-path; avoid layout-triggering animations.
- GSAP/Framer used selectively; tree-shake and lazy-init below-the-fold.
- Avoid WebGL unless a future decision justifies it.
